## Definitions and Notes
- *Positions gained/lost are based on a driverâ€™s race starting position versus finishing position, not qualifying result. Unclassified drivers do not receive positions-lost penalties but instead receive the -20 point DNF/Not classified penalty. Cars starting from the pit lane are treated as starting behind the last grid position.
